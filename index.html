Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. student in Department of Artificial Intelligence, at Xiamen University (XMU) under the supervision of Prof. Shaozi Li. I am also a joint Ph.D. student in Department of Medical Imaging and Medical Biophysics, Western University (UWO), London, Canada, under the supervision of Prof. Shuo Li. I received the B.S. degree from School of Computer Science, Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2014. My research interests include medical image analysis and computer vision.
